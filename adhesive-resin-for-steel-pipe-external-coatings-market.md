<p><p>The adhesive resin for steel pipe external coatings market includes various types, primarily thermoplastic coatings and fusion-bonded epoxy coatings. Thermoplastic coatings are flexible, durable, and can be easily reprocessed, providing excellent resistance to impact and chemicals. Fusion-bonded epoxy coatings involve applying a powdered epoxy that melts and fuses upon heating, creating a strong, impermeable barrier against corrosion. Both types enhance the lifespan of steel pipes in harsh environments, making them crucial for industries like oil and gas, construction, and utilities.</p></p>
