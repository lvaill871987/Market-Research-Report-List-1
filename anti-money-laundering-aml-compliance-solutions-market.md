These solutions are available in two primary market types: **Cloud-based** and **On-premise**. Cloud-based AML solutions are hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, cost-efficiency, and easy updates. On-premise solutions are installed locally on an organization's servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. Both types ensure regulatory compliance, but cloud-based systems are increasingly favored for their flexibility and accessibility in a rapidly evolving financial landscape.</p></p>
