<p><p>Architectural wall panels are versatile building materials used for insulation, aesthetics, and durability. **EPS Panels** (Expanded Polystyrene) are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer good thermal insulation. **PU Panels** (Polyurethane) provide superior insulation, moisture resistance, and structural strength, ideal for energy-efficient buildings. **Glass Wool Panels** are fire-resistant, soundproof, and eco-friendly, suitable for acoustic and thermal applications. **PF Panels** (Phenolic Foam) excel in fire safety, thermal performance, and durability. **Other panels** include materials like rock wool or composite options, catering to specific needs like enhanced fire resistance, sustainability, or design flexibility.</p></p>
