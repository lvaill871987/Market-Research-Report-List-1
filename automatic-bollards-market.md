<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Powered</li><li>Pneumatic Powered</li><li>Electric Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Bollards Market includes three main types based on power sources: Hydraulic Powered, Pneumatic Powered, and Electric Powered. Hydraulic bollards use fluid pressure for smooth, heavy-duty operation, ideal for high-security areas. Pneumatic bollards rely on compressed air, offering quick response times and durability in harsh environments. Electric bollards are powered by motors, providing precise control, energy efficiency, and quieter operation, suitable for urban and commercial spaces. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing performance, maintenance, and environmental adaptability.</p></p>
