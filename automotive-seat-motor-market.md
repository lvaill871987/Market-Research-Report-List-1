<p><ul><li>Four-pole motor</li><li>Bipolar motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seat motor market includes four-pole and bipolar motors, both used for seat adjustment systems. Four-pole motors feature four magnetic poles, offering smoother operation, higher torque, and better efficiency, making them ideal for premium vehicles. Bipolar motors have two poles, providing simpler design and cost-effectiveness, suitable for entry-level or mid-range cars. Both types cater to diverse automotive needs, balancing performance, durability, and affordability, with four-pole motors emphasizing advanced functionality and bipolar motors focusing on budget-friendly solutions.</p></p>
