<p><p>The Civil Aircraft Tire Market comprises two primary types: Radial Tires and Bias Tires. Radial tires feature layers of fabric cords arranged perpendicular to the tire's direction, offering better fuel efficiency, longer lifespan, and improved heat dissipation, making them suitable for modern aircraft. Bias tires have crisscrossed layers of fabric cords, providing robust sidewall strength and durability, ideal for smaller or older aircraft. Both types cater to specific operational needs, balancing performance, safety, and cost-effectiveness in aviation applications.</p></p>
