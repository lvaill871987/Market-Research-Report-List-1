<p><strong>The Coffee Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Pressed Oils</li><li>CO2/Solvent Extraction Oils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coffee oil market features primarily two types: cold pressed oils and CO2/solvent extraction oils. Cold pressed oils are obtained by mechanically pressing coffee beans, preserving their natural aroma and flavor without the use of heat, making them ideal for culinary applications. In contrast, CO2 and solvent extraction oils involve using pressurized carbon dioxide or chemical solvents to extract oil, resulting in higher yields and concentrated flavors. This method is often preferred for cosmetic and fragrance applications due to its efficiency.</p></p>
