<p><p>The commercial scales market is divided into two main types: mechanical scales and digital scales. Mechanical scales use a system of springs and levers to measure weight, providing a simple and reliable option, often preferred for its durability. On the other hand, digital scales employ electronic sensors for high accuracy and can display weight readings on a digital screen, often featuring advanced functionalities like connectivity and data management. Both types serve various industries, including retail, food service, and logistics.</p></p>
