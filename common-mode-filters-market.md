<p><ul><li>Split Winding</li><li>Bifilar Winding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Common mode filters are essential components used to suppress unwanted noise in electrical circuits. The market for these filters is segmented into split winding and bifilar winding types. Split winding filters utilize two separate windings to handle differential and common mode signals effectively, enhancing noise attenuation. In contrast, bifilar winding filters consist of closely coupled windings that improve magnetic coupling and minimize interference. Both types are crucial for ensuring signal integrity in various applications, including telecommunications and industrial electronics.</p></p>
