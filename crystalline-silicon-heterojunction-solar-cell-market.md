<p><p>The crystalline silicon heterojunction solar cell market comprises several technologies, notably screen printing low-temperature silver paste, smart-wire, and copper plating. Screen printing low-temperature silver paste is a conventional method for electrode formation, ensuring good conductivity while minimizing thermal stress. Smart-wire technology improves efficiency by using fine wires, reducing shading and material use. Copper plating is a cost-effective approach that replaces silver, providing high conductivity and reduced costs. These innovations enhance solar cell performance and production efficiency in the growing photovoltaic industry.</p></p>
