<p><strong>The Diamond-Impregnated Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sintering Type</li><li>Electroplating</li><li>Brazing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diamond-impregnated wheels market comprises three main types: sintering, electroplating, and brazing. Sintering involves compacting and heating diamond particles with a bonding material to create a durable wheel, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Electroplated wheels feature a layer of diamond particles adhered via an electrochemical process, offering precision and cost-effectiveness. Brazing involves joining diamond segments to a wheel core using a filler metal, providing strength and thermal conductivity. Each type serves specific industry needs, from construction to manufacturing.</p></p>
