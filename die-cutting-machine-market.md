<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Die Cutting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal-to-Metal</li><li>Perfing</li><li>Slitting</li><li>Kiss Cutting</li><li>Scoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die cutting machines are categorized based on their application techniques. Metal-to-metal die cutting involves cutting or shaping materials using metal dies that apply pressure. Perfing creates perforated lines for easy tearing. Slitting is the process of cutting materials into narrower widths. Kiss cutting refers to cutting through the upper layer of a material while leaving the lower layer intact. Scoring involves creating a crease in the material for easier folding or bending. Each type serves specific manufacturing needs in various industries.</p></p>
