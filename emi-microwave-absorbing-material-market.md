<p><ul><li>Low Permeability</li><li>High Permeability</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EMI microwave absorbing material market is categorized into low permeability and high permeability types. Low permeability materials are designed to absorb electromagnetic interference (EMI) by preventing magnetic field penetration, making them suitable for applications in environments where signal integrity is crucial. In contrast, high permeability materials enhance the absorption of microwave frequencies, effectively reducing EMI in high-frequency applications, such as in telecommunications and aerospace. Both types play essential roles in shielding and protecting electronic devices from undesired interference.</p></p>
