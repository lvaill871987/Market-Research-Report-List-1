<p><p>Epoxy concrete floor coatings are durable, protective layers applied to concrete surfaces. **Water-based** epoxies are eco-friendly, low-odor, and easy to clean, ideal for residential or light commercial use. **Solvent-based** epoxies offer superior durability and chemical resistance, suitable for industrial settings but emit strong fumes. **100% solids** epoxies contain no solvents or water, providing maximum strength, thickness, and chemical resistance, making them perfect for heavy-duty applications like warehouses or factories. Each type caters to specific needs based on durability, environmental impact, and application requirements.</p></p>
