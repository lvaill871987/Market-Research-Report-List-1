<p><strong>The Exhaust Gas Cleaning System(EGCS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Closed Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Mixed Waste Gas Purification System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>An Exhaust Gas Cleaning System (EGCS) is designed to reduce sulfur oxides (SOx) emissions from marine vessels, helping to comply with environmental regulations. The market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Open Loop EGCS** uses seawater to wash exhaust gases, discharging treated water back into the ocean.</p><p>2. **Closed Loop EGCS** recirculates water used in the process, treating it to remove pollutants, suitable for smaller seas.</p><p>3. **Mixed Waste Gas Purification System** combines both methods, optimizing emissions reduction across various conditions, supporting compliance and sustainability efforts.</p></p>
