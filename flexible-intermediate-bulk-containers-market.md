<p><p>The Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs) market consists of four main types: </p><p>- **Type A FIBCs** are standard sacks suitable for non-flammable materials. </p><p>- **Type B FIBCs** are designed to prevent electrostatic discharge for certain applications. </p><p>- **Type C FIBCs** feature conductive fabric for grounding, making them ideal for flammable powders. </p><p>- **Type D FIBCs** are built with anti-static properties to safely handle combustible materials without grounding. Each type serves specific industrial needs, ensuring safety and efficiency in bulk material handling.</p></p>
