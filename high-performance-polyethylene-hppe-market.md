<p><p>High Performance Polyethylene (HPPE) is categorized into three primary types based on density: Low Density (LDPE), Medium Density (MDPE), and High Density (HDPE). LDPE offers flexibility and impact resistance, making it ideal for packaging and film applications. MDPE combines strength and flexibility, suitable for containers and pipes. HDPE, known for its high strength-to-density ratio, is used in durable goods like bottles and industrial containers. Each type serves specific applications based on its unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
