<p><p>House wraps are protective barriers installed beneath exterior cladding to enhance building durability. Asphalt-impregnated paper or fiberglass wraps offer moisture resistance and strength. Micro-perforated wraps balance breathability and water resistance, ideal for humid climates. Spunbonded nonwoven wraps, made from synthetic fibers, provide durability and tear resistance. Woven wraps, crafted from woven polypropylene, offer high tensile strength. Drainable house wraps feature a textured surface to channel water away, preventing moisture buildup. Each type caters to specific climate and structural needs, ensuring energy efficiency and moisture management.</p></p>
