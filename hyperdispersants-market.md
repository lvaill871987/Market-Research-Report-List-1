<p><ul><li>Water-Borne Dispersants</li><li>Solvent-Borne Dispersants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hyperdispersants market is primarily categorized into two types: water-borne dispersants and solvent-borne dispersants. Water-borne dispersants are formulated to be mixed with water, making them suitable for various applications like paints and coatings, where a reduced environmental impact is desired. In contrast, solvent-borne dispersants are designed for use with organic solvents, offering effective dispersion in applications like inks and coatings where high performance and durability are critical. Both types enhance stability and prevent agglomeration of particles in formulations.</p></p>
