<p><p>The liquid filtration media market encompasses three primary types: mechanical, chemical, and biological medias. Mechanical medias physically strain out particles and sediments from liquids, often using filters made from materials like cloth or paper. Chemical medias involve reactions with contaminants to remove impurities, utilizing substances like activated carbon or ion exchange resins. Biological medias promote the growth of microorganisms to decompose organic matter, commonly used in wastewater treatment. Together, these medias improve water quality across various applications.</p></p>
