A 1:10 extraction means one part malachite is processed with ten parts solvent, yielding a highly concentrated product. 1:20 and 1:30 extractions are more diluted, with 20 and 30 parts solvent respectively, resulting in milder concentrations. These variations cater to diverse industrial needs, from cosmetics to pigments, based on desired potency and application requirements.</p></p>
