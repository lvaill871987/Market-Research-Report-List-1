<p><p>The Maritime Satellite Communications market includes two primary types: Very Small Aperture Terminal (VSAT) and Mobile Satellite Services (MSS). VSAT systems use small satellite dishes to provide high-speed broadband connectivity, ideal for large vessels requiring reliable data and voice communication. MSS, on the other hand, offers global coverage through satellite networks, catering to smaller vessels or remote areas with basic voice and data services. Both technologies enhance maritime operations, ensuring seamless communication, navigation, and safety at sea.</p></p>
