<p><p>The Medical Thermoplastic Elastomer market comprises several key types. Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU) are praised for their flexibility and durability, making them ideal for applications like catheters and seals. Styrene Block Copolymers (SBC) offer excellent impact resistance and are commonly used in various medical devices. Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV) combine the properties of rubber and plastic, providing superior thermal stability and chemical resistance, suitable for applications in medical tubing and diverse molded parts. Each type serves unique needs in medical applications.</p></p>
