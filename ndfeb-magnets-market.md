<p><ul><li>Sintered NdFeB Magnet</li><li>Bonded NdFeB Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NdFeB magnet market is primarily divided into two types: sintered and bonded NdFeB magnets. Sintered NdFeB magnets are created through a powder metallurgy process, yielding strong magnets suited for various applications, such as motors and generators. In contrast, bonded NdFeB magnets are produced by combining NdFeB powder with polymers, resulting in a flexible and lightweight magnet that is ideal for intricate shapes and applications like sensors and smaller electronic devices. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
