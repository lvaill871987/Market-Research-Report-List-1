<p><strong>The Oil & Gas Separator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravitational</li><li>Centrifugal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil and gas separator market comprises two primary types: gravitational and centrifugal separators. Gravitational separators rely on the natural separation of fluids based on density differences, allowing lighter components, such as gas, to rise while heavier ones, like oil and water, settle. In contrast, centrifugal separators employ high rotational speeds to create a force that enhances separation efficiency, effectively isolating different phases. Both types are essential in optimizing hydrocarbon recovery and maintaining production efficiency in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
