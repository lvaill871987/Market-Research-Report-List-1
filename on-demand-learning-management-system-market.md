<p><p>On-demand Learning Management Systems (LMS) can be categorized into public and private cloud markets. Public cloud LMS are hosted on shared infrastructure, offering scalable solutions accessible to a broad audience, often at lower costs. They benefit organizations seeking flexibility and quick deployment without extensive IT resources. In contrast, private cloud LMS are dedicated to a single organization, providing enhanced security, customization, and control over data. This option suits businesses with specific compliance needs or those prioritizing data privacy.</p></p>
