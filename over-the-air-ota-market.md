<p><ul><li>Firmware over-the-air (FOTA)</li><li>Software over-the-air (SOTA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Over the Air (OTA) refers to the wireless delivery of software updates, configurations, or applications to devices, enhancing functionality and performance. The Firmware Over-the-Air (FOTA) market focuses on updates to the underlying firmware of devices, ensuring optimal hardware performance and security. In contrast, the Software Over-the-Air (SOTA) market deals with application-level updates, providing end-users with new features or improvements to existing software. Both markets facilitate seamless device management, minimize downtime, and enhance the user experience.</p></p>
