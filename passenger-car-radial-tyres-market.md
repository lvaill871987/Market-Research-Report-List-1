These tyres are widely used in cars, SUVs, and other light passenger vehicles due to their superior performance, comfort, and durability.</p><p>The Passenger Car Radial Tyres Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing vehicle production, rising demand for fuel-efficient tyres, and advancements in tyre technology. Emerging markets in Asia-Pacific and Latin America are also playing a crucial role in driving global market growth.</p></p>
