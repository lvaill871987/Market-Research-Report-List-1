<p><p>The polyethylene plastic resins market primarily consists of two main types: Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) and High Density Polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE is characterized by its flexible and low-density structure, making it suitable for applications like plastic bags and films. Conversely, HDPE boasts a rigid and high-density configuration, offering greater strength and durability, ideal for products such as containers, pipes, and crates. Both types cater to diverse industries, including packaging, construction, and automotive, driving demand in the market.</p></p>
