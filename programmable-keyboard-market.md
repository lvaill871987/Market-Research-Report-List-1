<p><p>The programmable keyboard market comprises mainly two types: fully-programmable keyboards and macro programmable keyboards. Fully-programmable keyboards allow users to customize every key function, offering complete control over key assignments and functionalities. They are ideal for users who require extensive customization for various applications. In contrast, macro programmable keyboards enable users to set up specific sequences of actions or commands triggered by a single key press, catering to gamers and professionals who benefit from efficient workflow and enhanced productivity.</p></p>
