<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quartz Floor Market can be categorized into two main types: Press Molding and Casting Molding. Press Molding involves compressing quartz materials under high pressure to form durable and aesthetically pleasing flooring products, which are often used in residential and commercial spaces. Casting Molding, on the other hand, involves pouring a liquid resin mix containing quartz into molds, allowing for intricate designs and shapes once cured. Both methods are valued for their strength, design versatility, and resistance to wear and tear.</p></p>
