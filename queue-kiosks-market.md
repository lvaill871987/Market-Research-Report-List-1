<p><p>Queue kiosks are categorized into three main types: floor-standing, wall-mounted, and countertop. Floor-standing kiosks are free-standing units ideal for high-traffic areas, allowing users to access services without obstruction. Wall-mounted kiosks save space and are suited for smaller venues, typically enhancing customer flow by utilizing vertical areas. Countertop kiosks are compact and designed for use on surfaces like tables or counters, providing convenience for customers in settings such as retail or service environments. Each type enhances operational efficiency and customer experience.</p></p>
