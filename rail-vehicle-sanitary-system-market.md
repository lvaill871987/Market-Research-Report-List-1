<p><ul><li>Vacuum Toilet System</li><li>Pressured Water Flushing Toilet System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rail Vehicle Sanitary System market includes two primary types: Vacuum Toilet Systems and Pressured Water Flushing Toilet Systems. </p><p>Vacuum Toilet Systems utilize low water consumption and efficient waste removal through a vacuum mechanism, making them ideal for high-density passenger trains. </p><p>In contrast, Pressured Water Flushing Toilet Systems rely on a pressurized water supply to flush waste, offering a more traditional approach. These systems are typically found in older train models and provide a consistent flush experience, despite requiring more water.</p></p>
