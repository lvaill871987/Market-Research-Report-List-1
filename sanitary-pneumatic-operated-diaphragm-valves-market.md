<p><p>The sanitary pneumatic operated diaphragm valves market is segmented into three primary connection types: threaded, welded, and flanged. Threaded connection valves are easily installed and allow for simple maintenance, making them popular in smaller systems. Welded connection valves provide a permanent fit, ensuring leak-free operations in high-purity applications. Flanged connection valves offer robustness and easy disassembly for cleaning, often used in larger systems where frequent maintenance is required. Each type caters to specific industry needs in hygiene and efficiency.</p></p>
