<p><p>The sealing tape market can be categorized into single-layered and multi-layered types. Single-layered sealing tape consists of a single film or material layer, providing basic protection and adhesion, making it suitable for light-duty applications. In contrast, multi-layered sealing tape comprises several layers that enhance strength, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications, including industrial use and packaging sensitive materials. Both types cater to specific needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
