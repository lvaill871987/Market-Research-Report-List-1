<p><ul><li>Chemical Crosslinking</li><li>Physical Crosslinking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-healing hydrogels are advanced materials capable of autonomously repairing damage, mimicking biological tissues. In the market, they are categorized based on crosslinking methods. **Chemical crosslinking** involves covalent bonds, offering strong, stable networks but limited reversibility. **Physical crosslinking** relies on non-covalent interactions like hydrogen bonds or ionic forces, enabling dynamic, reversible healing but with weaker mechanical strength. Both types cater to diverse applications, from biomedical devices to soft robotics, balancing durability and adaptability to meet specific industry needs.</p></p>
