<p><strong>The Thermoplastic Engine Cover Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thermoplastic Engine Cover market primarily utilizes two manufacturing methods: Injection Molding and Casting. Injection Molding involves heating thermoplastic material to a molten state and injecting it into a mold under high pressure, creating precise, lightweight, and durable covers. Casting, on the other hand, involves pouring molten thermoplastic into a mold, allowing it to solidify, and producing covers with complex shapes and fine details. Both methods cater to automotive demands for heat resistance, strength, and design flexibility, with Injection Molding being faster and more cost-effective for high-volume production.</p></p>
