<p><strong>The Ultrahigh Vacuum (UHV) Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Getter Pumps (IGPs)</li><li>Titanium Sublimator Pumps (TSPs)</li><li>Non-evaporable Getters (NEG) Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrahigh Vacuum (UHV) pumps market includes various technologies essential for achieving and maintaining low pressure environments. Ion Getter Pumps (IGPs) utilize ionization to remove gases, making them effective for high-performance applications. Titanium Sublimator Pumps (TSPs) use titanium's reactive properties to capture contaminants, ideal for ultra-clean environments. Non-evaporable Getter (NEG) Pumps operate through chemical adsorption to eliminate gases without requiring a continuous supply of material, providing reliability and efficiency in UHV systems. Each type serves specific needs within research and industrial settings.</p></p>
