<p><p>The used and refurbished defibrillator market encompasses several key types. Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs) are portable devices designed for public use, providing critical care during cardiac emergencies. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) are surgically placed devices that monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks when needed. Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillators (WCDs) offer a non-invasive option for patients at risk of sudden cardiac arrest. Advanced Life Support (ALS) defibrillators are sophisticated machines used by medical professionals in emergency situations to provide comprehensive cardiac care.</p></p>
