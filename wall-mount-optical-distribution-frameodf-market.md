Drawer type ODFs feature sliding drawers for easy access and organization of fiber connections, ideal for medium-scale deployments. Modular type ODFs offer customizable configurations, allowing scalability and flexibility for large-scale or complex fiber networks. Each type caters to specific installation needs, balancing space efficiency, accessibility, and scalability in fiber optic management systems.</p></p>
